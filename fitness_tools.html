How do we calculate your caloric intake?

First we calculate your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate), then we multiply it to the following standards based on your activity level:
    Sedentary = BMR x 1.2 (little or no exercise, desk job)
    Lightly active = BMR x 1.375 (light exercise/ sports 1-3 days/week)
    Moderately active = BMR x 1.55 (moderate exercise/ sports 6-7 days/week)
    Very active = BMR x 1.725 (hard exercise every day, or exercising 2 xs/day)
    Extra active = BMR x 1.9 (hard exercise 2 or more times per day, or training for marathon, or triathlon, etc.)
